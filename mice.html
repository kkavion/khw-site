MICE Destinations

The seamless blend of modern amenities in a refined setting and the thrills of a spectacular destination will spark your senses and elevate your state of mind.

Alibaug

Mussoorie

Shimla

Udaipur

Be enchanted by its hemmed hills, azure lakes, rich history, fairy-tale palaces, temples, and grand architecture
