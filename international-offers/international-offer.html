Digha awaits your arrival, letting you escape the hustle and bustle of city life and spend a few precious moments in its sandy cradle.

One of the most popular beach destinations in West Bengal, Digha beckons travellers for a perfect weekend getaway. The translucent rays of the sun sparkle on the undulating surface of the sea, the waves crash over each other in a mad race to reach the powdery sands of the shoreline. You can lie on the sandy beach while sipping young coconut water under the shade of an umbrella. The smell of the salty sea permeates the air, interrupted only by the whiff of delicious seafood served on the shores and restaurants.

FAQs

What are the top tourist attractions in Gandhinagar?

Some of Gandhinagar's prominent tourist attractions are the Akshardham Temple, Indroda Nature Park, Sarita Udyan, Adalaj Stepwell, and Mahatma Mandir.

How do I get to Gandhinagar?

Gandhinagar is well-connected via road, rail, and air. The nearest airport is Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport in Ahmedabad, which is around 30 kilometers away. Gandhinagar has its own railway station, and buses run frequently to and from other major cities in Gujarat.

What is the best time to visit Gandhinagar?

The best time to visit is in the winter, between November and February, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing.

What are some popular festivals celebrated in Gandhinagar?

The prominent festivals celebrated in Gandhinagar are Uttarayan (Kite Festival), Navratri, Diwali, Holi, and the International Kite Festival.

What are the accommodation options in Gandhinagar?

Gandhinagar has a wide selection of accommodation options, from luxury hotels to economical guesthouses. Cambay Sapphire, Fortune Inn Haveli, and The Leela Gandhinagar are some of the most popular hotels.

What are the local cuisines and food specialties in Gandhinagar?

Gandhinagar offers a variety of Gujarati cuisine, including dishes like Dhokla, Thepla, Khandvi, and Fafda-Jalebi. Street food is also popular, with items like Pani Puri, Dabeli, and Bhajiya.

Is Gandhinagar a safe city for tourists?

Gandhinagar is considered one of the safest cities in India for tourists. However, it's always advisable to take general safety precautions.

What makes Gandhinagar different from other cities in Gujarat?

Gandhinagar is known for its planned layout, extensive green cover, and wide roads. Unlike many other Indian cities, it offers a more organized and serene environment.
