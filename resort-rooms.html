FAQs

What is Holiday Sale?

Holiday Sale is the sale of holiday packages comprising two, four, or six room nights at discounted rates at the beginning of the year. It can be purchased then and availed of any time during the year by a customer. You can now enjoy your holiday at affordable rates whenever you want!

Can I buy multiple packages?

Multiple packages can be bought at the same time and redeemed at different times of the year. You can now delight in many more holidays in your calendar year, we all love to make the most of sales after all!

Can I redeem two packages at the same time?

Yes, two packages can be redeemed at the same time but the reservations must be scheduled as per the instructions in the kit. They are subject to availability and should be reserved 7 days before visiting.

Can I redeem two kits consecutively?

Two kits can be redeemed consecutively provided you have made reservations for them 7 days prior. Since inventory is subject to availability, reservations cannot be made at the last minute but in advance, so the accommodation can be prepared for your stay.

Can I redeem my kit on premium days?

You can redeem your kit on premium days provided the premium surcharge is compensated for.

Can I club/combine different offers with my kit?

No additional offers or discount coupons can be combined with a Holiday Sale kit. Only the discount coupons received in the kit can be availed of at the time of redeeming your package.

Can I break my room night package and use each room night separately?

Yes, that can be done. However, it must be informed to the respective property prior to the reservation as accommodation is subject to availability.

Can I break my 4-room night package into two, of 2-room nights each and use it separately?

4 room night packages can be broken into two or more room nights and used separately. Before doing so, the property must be contacted and informed of the same so they can have the accommodation ready for you.

Can I break my 6-room night package into three, of 2-room nights each and use it separately?

Yes, that can very well be done, provided the property is informed of the same so that your accommodation can be prepared for you since it is subject to availability.

Can I break my 4-room night package into two and use it simultaneously by availing of 2 rooms?

2, 4 and 6 room night packages can be broken into smaller parts and utilized separately. If you wish to break a package and use it simultaneously by availing of two or more rooms, that can be arranged as well. However, it must be informed and reserved 7 days prior to the booking so that the accommodation can be prepared for you since it is always subject to availability.
