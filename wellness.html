Wellness Destinations

Put a stop to your racing mind and indulge in rejuvenating therapies to wear off your exhaustion and restore your inner peace!

Alleppey

The roaring waves of the sea breaking on the endless stretches of sandy beaches invite you to witness this beauty

Kasauli

Enchant yourself with rain-washed trees, radiant flowers, and cobbled forest paths, breathing in the fresh, clean air

Kollam

The land of a thousand shades will satiate your wanderlust with emerald seas, and coconut palm groves

Kottayam

The land of legends and lakes is a vast sea of culture, shrouded in emerald green and boasting a treasure trove of silk

Kovalam

Enshrouded by coconut and palm trees and outlined by the foamy waves of the Arabian Sea is this versatile beachside town

Kumarakom

This perfect splash of blue and green will satiate the wanderlust of your soul

Poovar

A Holy Grail to peace seekers, these shores are graced by the Arabian Sea, creating an image drawn by God's hand

Puducherry

The French Riviera of the East blends French aesthetics with Indian artistry in its hotels, restaurants, and shops

Shimla

The mystical embrace of oaks, cedars, pines and rhododendrons will entice you with its freshness and beauty

Udaipur

Be enchanted by its hemmed hills, azure lakes, rich history, fairy-tale palaces, temples, and grand architecture

Varkala

Experience nascent bohemian culture at the rare cliff, pristine waters that cleanse the soul & the thrill of adventure sports
