Bargain Holiday

Receive incredible offers at highly discounted rates!

Sometimes all we crave is a little more. A little more of the relaxing holidays and a little more of the attractive offers would motivate you to explore more. Bargain Holiday allows you the opportunity to receive incredible offers so you can explore a little more for a lot less.

How To Avail

1

This is an invitational offer for KKavion Encircle members only. You can become a member by filling in your details under Join The Club in the Encircle tab.

2

You can then place your request to avail of a Bargain Holiday by providing the requisite information under Your Query in the Bargain Holiday tab.

3

You would be offered a room night with a discount of up to 70% at any one of the participating properties via call and mail for a specific date.

4

The offer is only applicable for the date specified in your mail and must be availed of on short notice of 2 to 3 days by confirming with us.

5

The offer date cannot be rescheduled as it is subject to the availability of inventory.

6

The offer can be utilized by paying the requisite amount after which a Confirmation Voucher would be issued.

7

Please ensure that you provide the Confirmation Voucher at the time of Check-in to avail of the offer.

8

Do note that the offer is non-transferable and non-refundable.

FAQs

What is a Bargain Holiday?

A Bargain Holiday is an offer you receive by invite for specific dates when rates for room nights are heavily discounted. You can enjoy the benefits of a holiday while spending less too!

Who can avail Bargain Holiday?

Only a member of KKavion Encircle can avail of a Bargain Holiday. If you are wondering how to experience a holiday like this, you can head over to the Encircle tab, fill in your details and send your enquiry about it. You'd hear from the team shortly.

Which properties are offered through Bargain Holiday?

The properties where members would be offered a Bargain Holiday are those featured on the Bargain Holiday tab under Participating Properties.

How many rooms can be availed at the same time?

The number of rooms that can be availed at the same time differs from offer to offer and is subject to availability. It will be specified in the offer that you receive. If you require additional rooms, you can contact the team to inquire about them.

How many room nights can be availed?

The number of room nights is a part of the offer you receive and may vary. If you wish to prolong your stay, standard member rates for room nights will apply. This is subject to availability and a surcharge may be applied for premium days.

How many times can Bargain Holiday be availed in a year?

To avail of a Bargain Holiday all you need to do is place your request in the Your Query form of the Bargain Holiday tab. This can be done as many times as you please. However, because inventory is limited and subject to availability, you may not receive an offer every time you place a request.

What if I am not free on the day the offer is to be availed?

If you are not free on the date originally given, the offer can be rescheduled two times for alternate dates to help you avail of it. However, if even after rescheduling twice the offer is not used, it will lapse and you will have to place a new request to avail of it again.

Can I choose the property where I can avail of the offer?

The properties that are open to offering you a Bargain Holiday on those specific dates may be different from the one that you choose. Therefore, you are not allowed to select the property for your Bargain Holiday, but we assure you that you would have an equally wonderful time exploring new places.

Can I club/combine different offers with my Bargain Holiday?

No other offers or discount coupons can be clubbed/combined with an offer of a Bargain Holiday. Besides the discount given in the offer, the rest of the expenses are to be borne by the visitor.
