Encounter the wild

1

Camping & Glamping: Delight in cozy nights under the stars and savor meals by the fire. Explore lush trails and immerse yourself in rustic forest life while enjoying modern comforts like plush beds, stylish tents, and gourmet dining for a unique outdoor getaway.

2

Jeep Safari: Embark on an exhilarating journey as you navigate rugged trails, where you can encounter diverse wildlife up close, witness breathtaking landscapes, and experience the thrill of nature in its rawest form.

3

Boat Safari: Glide through calm waters on a thrilling boat safari, with your camera in hand to capture the fauna and the scintillating surroundings. Marvel at nature's wonders as you navigate serene lagoons and winding rivers.

4

Bird Watching: Watch colourful wings flit among the trees and listen to their melodious calls as avian wonders rejoice in their natural habitat.

Sundarbans

Explore the jungle, animals both unique and endangered, tribal life and an exclusive solidarity with nature's bounties
