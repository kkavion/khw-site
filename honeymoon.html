Experience the magic of a moment

1

Heritage: Explore ancient monuments, majestic palaces, and rich cultural sites to create romance amid timeless elegance. Witness iconic symbols of devotion and endurance as you craft your own tale of love.

2

Hill station: Savour a quiet pause with your beloved as you share your warmth amidst the chill. Wake up to misty mornings, take in the scenic view, trek through nature's bounties, and enjoy cozy moments by the fire to form a communion.

3

Backwater: Feel the gentle rhythm of your hearts align with each other and the serene waters as you soak in the lush landscape. Sail through the interlaced canals and lagoons while enjoying the luxurious warmth of your houseboat cruise.

4

Beach: Relish the ethereal beauty of your relationship against the backdrop of stunning sunsets, soft sands, and azure waters — enjoy relaxing in a tropical paradise.

Honeymoon Destinations

Embark on an intimate adventure encircled by stunning landscapes and luxurious stays!

Alleppey

The roaring waves of the sea breaking on the endless stretches of sandy beaches invite you to witness this beauty

Kanyakumari

India's last land, rich in mythology and culture, offers serenity at the confluence of three seas, amidst lush greenery

Kasauli

Enchant yourself with rain-washed trees, radiant flowers, and cobbled forest paths, breathing in the fresh, clean air

Kollam

The land of a thousand shades will satiate your wanderlust with emerald seas, and coconut palm groves

Kovalam

Enshrouded by coconut and palm trees and outlined by the foamy waves of the Arabian Sea is this versatile beachside town

Kumarakom

This perfect splash of blue and green will satiate the wanderlust of your soul

Mount Abu

This desert mirage, features rocky surfaces, rivers, waterfalls, lakes, and lush forests, creating a vibrant ecosystem

Mussoorie

Luxuriant oaks and pines and splendid scenery will usher you into a land where nature is all that matters

Poovar

A Holy Grail to peace seekers, these shores are graced by the Arabian Sea, creating an image drawn by God's hand

Shimla

The mystical embrace of oaks, cedars, pines and rhododendrons will entice you with its freshness and beauty

Udaipur

Be enchanted by its hemmed hills, azure lakes, rich history, fairy-tale palaces, temples, and grand architecture

Varkala

Experience nascent bohemian culture at the rare cliff, pristine waters that cleanse the soul & the thrill of adventure sports
