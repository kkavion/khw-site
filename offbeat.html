Uncover untrodden paths

1

Heritage: Explore ancient ruins, forgotten forts, exquisite temples — a land of colours and culture, an explosion of vibrant art, and a celebration of human existence.

2

Hill Station: Unearth untouched beauty in secluded retreats, hidden waterfalls, and scenic trails that let you enter into a world of fantasy.

3

Backwater: Mysterious lagoons, shrouded canals, and an endless network of lakes await your arrival to cruise through their serene waters.

4

Beach: An alcove ensconced by dense coconut groves, stunning beaches, and limitless seas welcomes you to soak in its unravaged beauty.

5

Urban Retreat: Nestled among hills and lakes, savour modern comforts while escaping the everyday hustle.

Offbeat Destinations

Experience the charm of unspoiled landscapes, concealed cultural gems, and unique adventures!

Alibaug

Resplendent with sandy beaches, crystal blue waters, and abundant fresh air is the perfect weekend getaway

Gandhinagar

Asia’s greenest capital blends culture, modernism & spirituality with its modern hubs and exquisite temples

Kasauli

Enchant yourself with rain-washed trees, radiant flowers, and cobbled forest paths, breathing in the fresh, clean air

Khandala

Escape the monotonous city life in the long, meandering streets, towering greenery, historical sites, and scenic beauty

Kollam

The land of a thousand shades will satiate your wanderlust with emerald seas, and coconut palm groves

Lavasa

Immerse yourself in glimpses of the Portofino of India and relish the ideal blend of infrastructure and nature

Mandarmani

Experience the sandy shores, the water sports and the serenity of the azure waters

Panvel

Panoramic views & intriguing historical remnants offer you the chance to unravel in nature and discover comfort and quiet

Panchgani

With strawberry farms and the fresh scent of flowers all around, the hill station invites you to relish nature’s bounty

Parwanoo

Nestled in the foothills of the Shivaliks, experience intimately the bounties of nature

Poovar

A Holy Grail to peace seekers, these shores are graced by the Arabian Sea, creating an image drawn by God's hand

Shantiniketan

Fall in love with the seamless blend of nature, culture and the arts in this quaint town

Varkala

Experience nascent bohemian culture at the rare cliff, pristine waters that cleanse the soul & the thrill of adventure sports

Yelagiri

Explore the untouched beauty of nature in the majestic waterfalls, the scenic gardens and the serene lakes
