FAQs

What is Bid & Win?

Bid & Win allows you to bid on the packages listed by the properties and give your own rate. If your bid happens to be the highest, you win the bid and can proceed to buy the package at a very exciting rate!

How to bid on a package?

All you need to do to participate is to click on Bid Now and fill in your details along with the bid amount. You will be informed via mail of the results of the bid.

Can I bid on multiple packages at the same time?

Bids are open to all so you can bid on multiple packages throughout the year!

If I do win the bid, when can I use the package?

The package is to be availed on the date specified on the bid. It cannot be rescheduled to another date as the inventory is subject to availability.

Can the amount I bid be the reserve price of the package?

The amount you bid needs to be above the reserve price of the package. Bidding the exact amount or a little bit higher could not always prove favourable. Bidding an amount that would be higher than the reserve price but still prove to extend a discounted rate to you would seem to be a wiser decision.

If I win more than one bid at the same time, can the reservations for them be spaced out and not back to back or at the same time?

Bids are open to being made for particular packages on specified dates. Choose the package you desire more since the dates cannot be rescheduled.
