Holiday Sale

Seize flexible holiday packages at greatly reduced rates!

Going on a holiday typically requires your booking to be confirmed for specific dates. What if that limitation did not exist? What if you could buy holiday packages and avail of it at any time of the year? Holiday Sale gives you the opportunity to buy stay packages in your resort of choice with the flexibility of dates and at rates that cannot be matched anywhere else!


How To Avail

1

This is offer is open to all. You can now buy packages comprising 2, 4 or 6 room nights at any of the participating properties with a discount of up to 50%!

2

You can place your request to receive an intimation about the Holiday Sale in advance by providing the requisite information under Your Query in the Holiday Sale tab.

3

When the sale commences, upto 2 packages can be bought by each customer or member.

4

A kit will be issued after receipt of payment towards the respective package.

FAQs

What is Holiday Sale?

Holiday Sale is the sale of holiday packages comprising two, four, or six room nights at discounted rates at the beginning of the year. It can be purchased then and availed of any time during the year. You can now enjoy your holiday at affordable rates whenever you want!

Who can avail Holiday Sale?

A Holiday Sale package can be bought by any visitor of the site wishing to avail of the offer.

Can I buy multiple packages?

Multiple packages can be bought at the same time and redeemed at different times of the year. You can now delight in many more holidays in your calendar year, we all love to make the most of sales after all!

Which properties are offered through Holiday Sale?

All the properties featured under Participating Properties on the Holiday Sale tab offer holiday packages at the time of sale.

Can I redeem two packages at the same time?

Yes, two packages can be redeemed at the same time but the reservations must be scheduled as per the instructions in the kit. They are subject to availability and should be reserved 7 days before visiting.

Can I redeem two kits consecutively?

Two kits can be redeemed consecutively provided you have made reservations for them 7 days prior. Since inventory is subject to availability, reservations cannot be made at the last minute but in advance, so the accommodation can be prepared for your stay

Can I redeem my kit on premium days?

You can redeem your kit on premium days provided the premium surcharge is compensated for.

Can I club/combine different offers with my kit?

No additional offers or discount coupons can be combined with a Holiday Sale kit. Only the discount coupons received in the kit can be availed of at the time of redeeming your package.
