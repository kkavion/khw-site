Pilgrimage

Indulge in challenging activities to invigorate your spirit and embrace the rush of life!

Indoor Games, Club Rio
Previous Next

Experience the thrill

1

Water Sports: Surf on the swelling tides at Varkala, jet ski and parasail in Alibaug's azure sea, or explore Kerala's serene backwaters with kayaking and houseboat cruises to soak in the thrill.

2

Aerial Activities: Paraglide over Yelagiri's lush valleys, zip line through Mussoorie's towering verdure, and the surreal environs of Kasauli and Parwanoo to feel unrestrained freedom in the endless skies.

3

Land Activities: Hike Yelagiri's trails, rappel in Mussoorie, rock climb in Pachmarhi, trek through Lonavala's caves and enjoy Matheran's scenic walks as you set out on your journey of exploration.

4

Winter Sports: Experience the unbound joy of tumbling with the snow during snow trekking on Mussoorie's scenic trails, skiing and snowboarding on Shimla's pristine slopes, and ice skating at The Ridge.
