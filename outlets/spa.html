Riversoul Spa

Riverside, Shibpur, Howrah, West Bengal, India


Indulge in the impeccable fusion of bucolic traditions and luxurious therapeutics from the world over!

Cosseted in the lap of the Sundra, Riversoul allows you to luxuriate in one of the most prestigious spas in town. Inspired by L’occitane, the French luxury brand will pamper you to your heart’s content with the help of their professional massage therapists. It will leave your body craving the profound peace and harmony the therapeutic experience confers.

Trivia

Riversoul Spa features a unique " Sundra Ritual" that incorporates local herbal ingredients and river water to create a deeply refreshing and spiritual experience.

Good To Know

The spa’s serene riverside location enhances relaxation along with traditional Indian therapies, including aromatherapy, Ayurvedic massages, and rejuvenating rituals.

Ambience Mediterranean

Spread over an area of 14000 sq feet, this Mediterranean wellness sanctuary takes you on a journey of tranquillity, with its grand and opulent space and Mediterranean ambience. Your mind will be hushed by the serenity as you step through the doors. The soft amber lights will warm your skin and the beautiful fragrance of jasmine and orchid will push you to experience the sensorial trail designed by Riversoul. It will make you want to cherish the comfort and luxury in the arms of nature.

Therapy International

Immerse yourself in the international therapeutic experience at Riversoul, providing you with the touch of brilliance from every node of the longitude to rejuvenate every inch of your skin. Bask in the magic potions of L’occitane’s Immortelle for your radiance, hand-crafted by professional therapists exclusively for your skin. Dive into this enriching therapeutic session of relaxing massages, scrubs and treatments which your body has been longing for.

Signature Treatments

Hawaiian LomiLomi of Riversoul is an enriching and therapeutic massage treatment passed down from Hawaii performed by professional therapists. Firm yet gentle caresses from your trapezius, latissimus dorsi, rhomboids to your erector spinae will have you savouring the comfort and relaxation their professionalism entails. A deep sense of elevation, a new high is felt during the experience that will make you keep coming back to feel the same way again.
