Sonar Bangla Bhoj

Riverstone Retreat

Riverside, Shibpur, Howrah, West Bengal, India


Revel in the magnificence of old Kolkata through every sight, scent, sound and taste!

Riverstone Retreat seems to have gotten the old saying of pleasing the heart through the stomach just right! From the delicious food served as room service to traditional Bengali cuisine at Sonar Bangla Bhoj, authentic oriental cuisine at Zen Palate and other multi-cuisine restaurants, cafes and lounges, Riverstone Retreat is all set to pave its way to your heart.

Trivia

The restaurant's name, "Sonar Bangla Bhoj," is inspired by the famous poem by Rabindranath Tagore, symbolizing a golden boat and evoking a sense of nostalgia and tradition.

Good To Know

Sonar Bangla Bhoj's decor reflects the rich cultural heritage of Bengal, providing an immersive dining experience that celebrates local art and history.

Ambience Mediterranean

Sonar Bangla Bhoj is a manifestation of the authentic Bengali culture. Not only will it gratify you with its rich Bengali food but also mesmerize you with its décor and view. The old rustic railings, ancient Bengali style bed, lamps, ‘haath pakhaa,’ tables made from old wooden windows and jewellery boxes, ‘kashar’ (brass) plates and cutleries will imbue in the atmosphere the charm of old Kolkata.

You can also choose to dine within the proximity of the Ganges and bask in the beauty of the surreal sunset being reflected in the river. The gentle fragrance of fresh flowers compliments the strong but enticing aroma of the exquisite Bengali Thali and the decorative ‘noukas’ take you back to your roots. The experience of dining here will impose upon you a renewed sense of sound, smell, taste, touch and a sight that is bound to etch this very view to memory!

Cuisine International

The quintessential Bengali thali with all its glorious entrée in cheerful hues served here will connect your soul and stomach to the traditional Bengali roots that will have you savouring each delicacy. It will satiate your cravings with its wide variety of dishes ranging from the rich and spicy ‘Kosha Mangsho’, the subtle and soothing ‘Sorse Ilish Bhapey’ to the Chanar Payesh and Bhapa Sandesh which will not only overwhelm you with their sweetness but have you craving more with their distinct flavour!

Signature Dishes

Sonar Bangla Bhoj’s zamindari thali quite certainly caters to a people that have a soft spot for their fishes and its various preparations with their numerous varieties of fresh fishes and its gravies. The steamed Bhetki Paturi wrapped in banana leaf alongside the fragrant Sugandhi Gobindhobog is sure to be a favourite among many! If this already has you salivating then the motley assortment of desserts will only leave the “sweet-tooth” in you ecstatic!
