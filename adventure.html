Experience the thrill

1

Water Sports: Surf on the swelling tides at Varkala, jet ski and parasail in Alibaug's azure sea, or explore Kerala's serene backwaters with kayaking and houseboat cruises to soak in the thrill.

2

Aerial Activities: Paraglide over Yelagiri's lush valleys, zip line through Mussoorie's towering verdure, and the surreal environs of Kasauli and Parwanoo to feel unrestrained freedom in the endless skies.

3

Land Activities: Hike Yelagiri's trails, rappel in Mussoorie, rock climb in Pachmarhi, trek through Lonavala's caves and enjoy Matheran's scenic walks as you set out on your journey of exploration.

4

Winter Sports: Experience the unbound joy of tumbling with the snow during snow trekking on Mussoorie's scenic trails, skiing and snowboarding on Shimla's pristine slopes, and ice skating at The Ridge.

Adventure Destinations

Conquer the mountains, the waters and the skies, overcome your fears and liberate yourself in your adventure holidays!

Alibaug

Resplendent with sandy beaches, crystal blue waters, and abundant fresh air is the perfect weekend getaway

Alleppey

The roaring waves of the sea breaking on the endless stretches of sandy beaches invite you to witness this beauty

Digha

Escape the hustle and bustle of city life and spend a few precious moments in this sandy cradle

Kasauli

Enchant yourself with rain-washed trees, radiant flowers, and cobbled forest paths, breathing in the fresh, clean air

Kollam

The land of a thousand shades will satiate your wanderlust with emerald seas, and coconut palm groves

Kumarakom

This perfect splash of blue and green will satiate the wanderlust of your soul

Lonavala

The serene surroundings and misty clouds will elevate you to the ethereal

Matheran

Shaded forests, cobbled paths lined with tall coniferous trees & stunning lookouts open up the untouched world of nature

Mussoorie

Luxuriant oaks and pines and splendid scenery will usher you into a land where nature is all that matters

Panchmarhi

Lush greenery, wildlife, sparkling waterfalls, & hilltop views offer a secluded, magical escape for complete relaxation

Parwanoo

Nestled in the foothills of the Shivaliks, experience intimately the bounties of nature

Shimla

The mystical embrace of oaks, cedars, pines and rhododendrons will entice you with its freshness and beauty

Varkala

Experience nascent bohemian culture at the rare cliff, pristine waters that cleanse the soul & the thrill of adventure sports

Yelagiri

Explore the untouched beauty of nature in the majestic waterfalls, the scenic gardens and the serene lakes
