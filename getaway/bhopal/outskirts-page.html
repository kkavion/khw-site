Other Attractions

Van Vihar National Park

An urban sanctuary for wildlife and lush green trails.

Upper Lake (Bhojtal)

A serene water haven perfect for boating and scenic views.

Manbhavan Tekri

A tranquil retreat from the city hustle, this hill offers stunning views and a historic temple.

Sanchi Stupa

A UNESCO heritage site, this ancient Buddhist stupa offers profound historical and spiritual insights.

Shaukat Mahal and Sadar Manzil

Designed in the Indo-Saracenic style, these historical buildings showcase Bhopal's royal heritage.

Bhimbetka Rock Shelters

Prehistoric rock shelters with intriguing ancient cave paintings.

Madhya Pradesh Tribal Museum

Rich exhibits showcasing the diverse tribal cultures of Madhya Pradesh.

Bhojeshwar Temple

A massive, mysteriously unfinished lingam and rich architectural details are what make this ancient Shiva temple famous.

Taj-ul-Masajid

Taj-ul-Masajid: A majestic mosque known for its stunning architecture and grandeur.
