Other Attractions

Lalbagh Botanical Garden

Perfect for nature lovers is this lush and expansive garden with rare flora, serene lakes and a glasshouse.

Bannerghatta National Park

A thrilling haven for wildlife enthusiasts, this park lets you go on safaris and wander through the zoo & butterfly park.

Nandi Hills

An ancient Nandi temple, mysterious trails and splendid sunrises make up this scenic hill station.

Savandurga

An ideal spot for trekking & rock climbing, this is one of Asia's largest monoliths with a striking view to offer.

Bangalore Palace

Tudor-style architecture, lush gardens, and regal interiors characterise this remarkable palace.

Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace

A charming wooden palace that is a legacy of intricate carvings and Indo-Islamic architecture.

ISKCON Temple

A vibrant milieu of spirituality and culture in a grand temple complex dedicated to Lord Krishna.

Bull Temple

With a massive Nandi bull statue & Dravidian architecture, this ancient temple is highly revered.

Halasuru Someshwara Temple

Displaying exquisite Dravidian architecture and a rich cultural heritage, this historic temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva.
