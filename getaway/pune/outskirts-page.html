Other Attractions

Okayama Friendship Garden (Pu La Deshpande Udyan)

Manicured landscapes, koi ponds, and tranquil pathways adorn this Japanese-style garden.

Mulshi Lake and Dam

A scenic getaway, a stunning lake surrounded by lush greenery perfect for picnics & boating.

Rajiv Gandhi Zoological Park

A well-maintained zoo with diverse wildlife, a snake park, and a tranquil lake.

Shinde Chhatri

Popular for its intricate architecture & historic significance, this is a grand memorial dedicated to the Maratha leader Mahadji Shinde.

Sinhagad Fort

A historic hilltop fort with panoramic views, trekking trails, and rich Maratha history.

Aga Khan Palace

Now a Gandhi memorial, this is a majestic palace with beautiful gardens and a history connecting it to India's freedom struggle.

Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum

Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum: An eclectic collection of artifacts, musical instruments, art, and everyday objects from Indian history is showcased in this unique museum.

All Saints Church

A charming 19th-century church in Pune known for its Gothic architectural style & stained-glass windows.
