Other Attractions

Sabarmati Riverfront

An urban oasis along the Sabarmati, this riverfront offers serene promenades, parks, and cultural spaces.

Kankaria Lake

The perfect space for family outings and leisure — a scenic artificial lake with a zoo, amusement park, and boat rides.

Bhadra Fort

A historic legacy containing a fort with intricate architecture, a temple, palace, and lush courtyard.

Statue of Unity

A global attraction — this statue of Sardar Patel is the world's tallest statue and a symbol of unity.

Bai Harir Ni Vav

With intricate Indo-Islamic architecture, this 5-tiered stepwell is steeped in rich history.

Hutheesing Jain Temple

A stunning reflection of Jain architecture, this exquisitely carved temple is dedicated to Lord Dharmanatha.

Swaminarayan Temple

With a serene spiritual ambience, this ornate temple displaying intricate wood work is dedicated to Swaminarayan.

Jama Masjid

Built in 1424, this impeccable mosque with a spacious courtyard is designed in Indo-Saracenic architecture.

Jhulta Minara Sidi Bashir Mosque

A marvel of medieval engineering, this incredible mosque has swaying minarets.
