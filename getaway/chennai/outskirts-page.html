Other Attractions

Guindy National Park

An oasis of diverse wildlife, serene trails, and children's park in an urban setting.

Arignar Anna Zoological Park

With an offering of safaris, nature walks, and diverse fauna, this is one of India's largest zoos.

Marina Beach

Golden sands, bustling stalls and stunning sunrises, this is India’s longest urban beach.

Elliot’s Beach (Besant Nagar Beach)

Savour the sea breeze and evening walks at this tranquil beach.

Fort St. George

A heritage of the British colonial past with a museum, church, and government buildings.

Government Museum

A trove of extensive art collections, and a exhibition of archaeology & natural history.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

A temple with an exquisite gopuram designed in the Dravidian-style and dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Santhome Cathedral and Basilica

A sacred pilgrimage site, a Gothic-style church built over the tomb of St. Thomas.

Vivekananda House

The dwelling of Swami Vivekananda, now a museum dedicated to his life and teachings.

Dakshina Chitra

A montage of South Indian heritage displaying traditional homes, crafts, and cultural exhibits.
