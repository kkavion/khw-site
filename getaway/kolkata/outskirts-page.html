Other Attractions

Botanical Garden

With an iconic Great Banyan Tree, this lush garden offers diverse flora & serene walks.

Eco Park

A green escape in the heart of New Town, this sprawling urban park has themed gardens, boating, and recreation.

Princep Ghat

A grand colonial pavilion & a scenic riverside promenade offering serene boat rides.

Marble Palace

A stunning 19th-century mansion adorned with exquisite marble, housing a rich collection of art, antiques, and sculptures.

Indian Museum

India’s oldest museum, boasting extensive collections of art, archaeology, and natural history, showcasing Kolkata's rich heritage.

Victoria Memorial

A symbol of Kolkata’s colonial past, an iconic white marble monument surrounded by lush gardens, commemorating Queen Victoria.

Belur Math

A serene riverside temple complex and headquarters of the Ramakrishna Mission, blending Hindu, Christian, and Islamic architectural styles.

Parshwanath Temple

An ornate Jain temple known for its intricate glass mosaics, peaceful ambiance, and spiritual significance.

Dakshineshwar Kali Temple

This revered riverside temple of Goddess Kali is a major pilgrimage site associated with the mystic Ramakrishna.

Birla Mandir

With intricate carvings and a serene spiritual atmosphere, this grand marble temple is dedicated to Lord Krishna.

St. Paul's Cathedral

A landmark of Kolkata's colonial heritage, a majestic Gothic Revival cathedral with stained glass windows and a peaceful garden.

Jorasanko Thakur Bari

The ancestral home of Rabindranath Tagore, now a museum, offering insights into the poet's life and the Bengal Renaissance.
