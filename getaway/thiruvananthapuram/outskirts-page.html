Other Attractions

Neyyar Wildlife Sanctuary

A natural haven of variant flora and fauna with boating, trekking, and a lion safari amidst lush landscapes.

Kovalam Beach

Crescent-shaped shores, calm waters, and iconic lighthouse views, make this beach perfect for relaxation and water sports.

Varkala Cliff and Beach

A stunning beach flanked by red cliffs & dotted by wellness hubs offering panoramic ocean views.

Ponmudi Hills

Misty peaks, lush tea plantations, and winding trails will greet you here as you trek your way through.

Kuthiramalika Palace

This historic palace with intricate wooden carvings houses royal artifacts, that offer insights into Kerala's royal heritage.

The Kanakakkunnu Palace

A grand palace turned cultural hub, this regal house hosts events and sheds light on Kerela's royal past.

Napier Museum

This striking Indo-Saracenic building is a storehouse of art, archaeology, and natural history.

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

A Dravidian cultural legacy, this hallowed shrine is devoted to Lord Vishnu.

Vettukad Church (Madre de Deus Church)

Attracting thousands of devotees, this colossal church steeped in history is known for its annual festival.
