FAQs

Are rooms included in these day out packages?

The day out packages comprise activities, therapies or dining options. Rooms are not included, however, they are subject to availability and can be booked at standard rates

What is the difference between Day Out and Residential Packages?

Day Out packages consist of activities, meals, therapies or special dining options specific to the type of package selected whereas residential packages include only accommodation/rooms.

Can I stay beyond the checkout time?

It is essential to check out at the specified time. If you wish to stay beyond it for any reason or choose to explore any other property offerings, you must contact the front office and state your query. Upon their affirmation, you are free to remain at the property and then leave at the revised checkout time.

Are there set food options for Food Fiesta or can they be customized?

The cuisine will differ from property to property but will be specified in the package, as will the rest of the offerings. The food items may or may not be detailed in the package depending upon each property. The packages are created to make day outs fun and affordable which limits the option of customizing the food items. However, all the properties showcased sure do serve great meals, so we hope you can enjoy what each property brings to your platter!

Can I select the therapies I want or are they pre-decided according to the package?

Therapies are pre-decided according to the package. All the included treatments will be specified in the offerings. If you wish to opt for an additional therapy or two, standard rates will apply and it is also subject to availability.

Are the food options fixed for Celebration and Corporate packages or can they be customized?

Meal offerings are pre-decided for all packages, all made with the promise of allowing you to have a good experience on your memorable days! Fret not, and let the properties you select guide your palate with the most flavourful dishes! Sizeable groups opting for Corporate packages can customize the cuisine and food items by getting in touch with us or the property directly.
