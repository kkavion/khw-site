Other Attractions

Yamuna Biodiversity Park

An ideal spot for nature walks and birdwatching, this is a green oasis dedicated to preserving native flora and fauna.

India Gate

Set amidst sprawling lawns, this is a magnificent war memorial honouring Indian soldiers.

Qutub Minar

A UNESCO World Heritage site, this iconic 12th-century minaret, the tallest in India, showcases exquisite Indo-Islamic architecture.

Vishwa Shanti Stupa

A beacon of peace, this white-domed stupa set amidst lush gardens promotes global harmony.

Red Fort

Massive red sandstone walls and palatial interiors characterise this grand Mughal fort which is a UNESCO World Heritage.

Humayun’s Tomb

This stunning Mughal mausoleum with Persian architecture and symmetrical gardens is considered a precursor to the Taj Mahal.

Swaminarayan Akshardham Temple

The intricate carvings of this grand temple reflect ancient India's culture, spirituality, and architecture.

Chhatarpur Temple

Dedicated to Goddess Katyayani, this sprawling temple complex in South Delhi blends traditional and modern architecture.

Lotus Temple

A Bahá'í House of Worship with striking lotus-shaped architecture, promoting unity and peace.
