Signature Treatment Shirodhara

Stress has become a part of our lifestyle, and while Sanjeeva Spa might not be able to take that all away their signature treatment ‘shirodhara' makes attempts to sooth it. ‘Shiro' means liquid, ‘dhara' means flow and as its name suggests, it is a therapy which involves a mixture of essential oil being poured on the forehead while you lay flat on clinically designed beds. This healing process is followed by an immaculate head massage by their experts which facilitates the blood flow in your head.
