Ambience Contemporary

Reflections will charm you, not only with the food they serve but also with the impressive view of the Ganges! The beige theme, bricked wall and large mirrors make the space look lavish. What heightens the effect are the large glass panels that overlook the Ganges, making it seem as expansive as the sky and as endless as the waters.

Cuisine Multi-cuisine

Relishing spaghetti in white sauce with charcoal chicken while sipping their Mint Special and watching the setting sun surely takes the dining experience a notch higher. If you prefer noodles or burgers, well, all there is to say is that they can cater to everything that you like!

Signature Dish Zamindari Thali

Sonar tori's zamindari thali quite certainly caters to a people that have a soft spot for their fishes and its various preparations with their numerous variety of fresh fishes and its gravies. The steamed Bhetki Paturi wrapped in banana leaf alongside the fragrant Sugandhi Gobindhobog is sure to be a favourite among many! If this already has you salivating then the motley assortment of desserts will only leave the “sweet-tooth” in you ecstatic!
