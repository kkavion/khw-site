Ambience

Spread over an area of 14000 sq feet, this Mediterranean wellness sanctuary takes you on a journey of tranquillity, with its grand and opulent space and Mediterranean ambience. Your mind will be hushed by the serenity as you step through the doors. The soft amber lights will warm your skin and the beautiful fragrance of jasmine and orchid will push you to experience the sensorial trail designed by Anaya. It will make you want to cherish the comfort and luxury in the arms of nature.

You can choose to spend your time on the deck to take in the natural environs or gather with your close ones in the enclosure below. Planning events for special occasions here will offer a unique blend of celebrating your joys right in the midst of nature. The waves gently crashing by the Pari as you groove to the beats of music within the enclave will be a surreal experience!

Food Personalised

You can choose to have your food catered from any of the fine-dining outlets in The Ffort Raichak. Whether you crave hors d'oeuvre of several varieties or a specific cuisine that suits your palate, prepare to be blown away by the flavours that will burst on your tongue. From Italian Pizzas to Chinese dumplings or Indian kebabs, the offerings are sure to leave the epicurean in you is sated.
