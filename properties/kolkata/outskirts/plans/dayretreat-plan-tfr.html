Attractions

Going to a resort and doing nothing at all is barely any fun so Raichak provides you with all that you need to amuse yourself! From lounges where you can groove to the lively music to playing games in a club overlooking the Ganges or going for a riverside cruise, you have all that you need to keep yourself happily occupied and spend a fun-filled day with your loved ones!!
